About & Contact

Ultimately, philosophy, with all its rows of thick, boring books, is nothing more than a continuation of the naive childish questions of "why me," "what is this," and "why?" Over time, curiosity fades: people recover from the Cogito virus by maturing and weaning themselves off thinking about things unrelated to everyday life. Some people are content with simple answers, while others go further—like Vincent.

ERGO PROXY SUM is designed for those who prefer to dig into everything themselves, and "Ergo Proxy" is the very trail from which a very interesting journey can begin.
